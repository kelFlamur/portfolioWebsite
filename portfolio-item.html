A project for Life Directions.A Youth Group

A Capstone Project

Life Directions was a non-for-profit company that served under privileged city children. Their goal was to inspire young adults through religion.

Our group was tasked with creating a mobile app that allowed these young adults to keep track of their bible scripture readings for 9 days will they were out on a mission trip

To do this our group used react native with redux, and firebase. It allowed users to track in, create a profile, and see their progress in real time. I was responsible for a large portion of UI and navigation.
